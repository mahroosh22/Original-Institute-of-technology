Who We Are

Oriental Institute of Technology, Mandi Bahauddin is a premier private technical institute offering high-quality diploma programs to matric-pass students across Punjab.

We offer a range of programmes including DAE Civil Technology, DAE Electrical Technology, Matric Technical, AutoCAD, Civil Surveyor, and Quantity Surveyor — designed to equip students with industry-relevant technical skills and practical knowledge.

Our institute is registered by PSDA and affiliated with PBTE Lahore, ensuring nationally recognized qualifications that open doors to rewarding careers in Pakistan and abroad.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education that transforms young graduates into skilled, disciplined, and job-ready professionals — ready to serve Pakistan's growing industrial sector.

🌟

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and respected technical institute in Mandi Bahauddin — producing graduates who lead with competence, integrity, and professional excellence.
